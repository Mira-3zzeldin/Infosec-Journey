## 📈 Learning Strategy & Methodology

**To master the shadows, one must first understand the light 🌓** ... My approach to **Offensive Security** is rooted in the "Core-First" principle: mastering system architecture and protocols before exploitation. I believe that a tool is only as effective as the operator's grasp of the underlying technology.

- **Deep-Dive Synthesis :** Transitioning from neural mapping ( pen & paper ) to digital documentation.
- **Standardized Documentation :** Labs follow a strict Objective → Methodology → Evidence pipeline.
- **Operational Validation :** Proving theories through hands-on labs and continuous refinement.
